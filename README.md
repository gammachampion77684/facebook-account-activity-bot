# Facebook Account Activity Automation with MadHub

MadHub's powerful automation features enable you to manage your Facebook account activity seamlessly and efficiently. With capabilities such as **Facebook Account Warm-Up** and **Facebook Auto-Post**, MadHub allows you to nurture your account health and enhance its visibility on the platform. This comprehensive suite of tools is designed to ensure your activities align with best practices, promoting engagement while minimizing risks.

## Feature Overview

### Facebook Account Warm-Up
The **Facebook Account Warm-Up** feature in MadHub is an essential tool for simulating real user behavior. This feature intelligently browses your homepage feeds and video pages, skipping ad videos while allowing for likes and comments. You can configure interaction probabilities and execution distribution, providing a tailored approach to nurturing your account. By gradually increasing your activity level, you can enhance account trust and reduce the risk of bans—making it ideal for new account nurturing or maintaining existing accounts.

### Facebook Auto-Post
MadHub’s **Facebook Auto-Post** feature allows users to automatically publish content on their personal homepage. This feature supports various modes, including input mode and file mode, enabling flexible content management. You can set posting intervals, define the number of posts to make, and control visibility options (public or friends-only). This capability ensures consistent content delivery, essential for maintaining engagement and visibility on the platform.

## How to Use MadHub Features

### Utilizing Facebook Account Warm-Up
1. **Configuration Steps**: 
   - Access the MadHub interface and select **Facebook Account Warm-Up**.
   - Set the **interaction probability** to determine how often the feature engages with posts.
   - Adjust the **execution probability distribution** to control which types of interactions (likes, comments) occur more frequently.
   - Start the warm-up process, allowing MadHub to simulate natural browsing behavior.

2. **Parameter Settings**:
   - **Interaction Probability**: A range of 30-70% is recommended for gradual warming.
   - **Execution Distribution**: Customize the frequency of likes versus comments based on your interaction strategy.

### Implementing Facebook Auto-Post
1. **Configuration Steps**:
   - Navigate to the **Facebook Auto-Post** section in MadHub.
   - Choose between **input mode** for manual content or **file mode** for bulk uploads.
   - Define the **posting schedule**, ensuring regular updates to your homepage. 
   - Opt for content rotation to prevent repetitive posts, enhancing user engagement.

2. **Parameter Settings**:
   - **Operation Interval**: Set this to every 1-2 hours for optimal visibility.
   - **Single Post Count**: Adjust to ensure a consistent flow of content (e.g., 3-5 posts per session).
   - **Visibility Options**: Select according to your audience engagement strategy (public or friends-only).
